Welcome to the **Talkover API** – a powerful platform that enables AI-driven phone conversations, helping businesses automate and scale customer communication through voice.

With Talkover, you can:

- Build natural, real-time call flows powered by LLMs (Large Language Models)
- Automate support, sales, scheduling, and more
- Operate 24/7 with no human intervention
- Maintain full control and transparency over every call

## Two Powerful Ways to Make Calls

Talkover offers two distinct approaches to customer engagement, each optimized for different business needs:

### **Individual Agent Calls** - Immediate, High-Impact Engagement
Perfect for **instant, personalized customer interactions** that drive immediate results:

- **Instant Execution**: Calls made immediately without waiting for scheduling
- **Real-Time Context**: Immediate follow-ups after form submissions or customer actions
- **High-Value Conversions**: Ideal for high-priority leads and urgent customer needs
- **Flexible Parameters**: Customize call parameters on-the-fly for specific situations

**Best for**: Form follow-ups, immediate customer service, urgent scheduling, high-priority leads, and any situation requiring instant response.

### **Campaign Calls** - Strategic, Automated Outreach
Designed for **systematic, large-scale customer engagement** with advanced automation:

- **Campaign-Specific Settings**: Inherits campaign configuration (retry logic, cooldown periods, call hours)
- **Advanced Scheduling**: Respects business hours, timezone settings, and day restrictions
- **Retry Logic**: Built-in retry mechanisms with configurable cooldown periods
- **Performance Analytics**: Comprehensive tracking and reporting at the campaign level
- **Compliance Features**: Do-not-call list management and regulatory compliance tools

**Best for**: Customer outreach campaigns, lead nurturing, appointment reminders, market research, and any scenario requiring consistent, automated calling with sophisticated rules.
